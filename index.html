Free Limited Sync Services for Environmental Supporters

As part of our commitment to environmental sustainability, we provide free limited sync services to environmental supporters. To qualify, supporters must submit proof of certification, such as certificates from recognized environmental organizations (e.g., WWF, Greenpeace), evidence of participation in eco-activities (e.g., tree-planting events or clean-up campaigns), or a letter of recommendation from a verified environmental group.
